The Construction & Maintenance Electrican Pre-Apprenticeship training program is designed to give hands-on training for individuals looking to work in the electrical field within a residential, commercial or industrial setting.

There is consistent demand for Construction & Maintenance Electrican Pre-Apprenticeship gradautes in London, Thamesford, Lucan, Strathroy, St. Thomas, Ingersoll, Stratford, Kitchener-Waterloo, Brampton, Toronto, Mississauga, Scarborough, Milton, Burlington, Oakville, Hamilton and other cities across southern Ontario.
